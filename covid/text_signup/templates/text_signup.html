About

In these unprecedented times, COVID-19 cases continue to grow in America. From cities to rural towns, stay informed on where COVID-19 is spreading to understand how it could affect families, commerce, and travel. Follow new cases found each day and the total number of cases and deaths in the US.

The data is retrieved from USAFacts. Check out their site for more information. They pull information daily from individual state websites and perform some manual data collection as well.

How it works:
